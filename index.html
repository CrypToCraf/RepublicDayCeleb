Republic Day Celebration

About Republic Day

Republic Day honors the date on which the Constitution of India came into effect on 26 January 1950.

National Flag

The Indian national flag consists of three colors: saffron, white, and green, representing courage, truth, and peace.

Celebrations

Republic Day is celebrated with parades, patriotic songs, and various cultural events across the country.
